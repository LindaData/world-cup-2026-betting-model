Local film workflow

How To Mark A Match As Recorded

Record the match with your own tool, save it into the private recordings folder, then rerun the local registry and render steps. The public site only shows whether local film exists. It does not publish the file or its full path.

1. Save the filedata/private/recordings/
2. Use the match keywc2026-49478__south-africa-vs-canada.mp4
3. Rebuild the registryR/26_build_local_recording_registry.R
Show local refresh commands 
& 'C:\Program Files\R\R-4.1.1\bin\Rscript.exe' 'R/26_build_local_recording_registry.R'
& 'C:\Program Files\R\R-4.1.1\bin\Rscript.exe' 'R/25_build_game_archive.R'
& 'C:\Program Files\R\R-4.1.1\bin\Rscript.exe' 'R/12_render_reports.R'
Workflow

How To Use The Archive

Use the registry before kickoff to find the official provider listing. After the match, use the review board to see whether the model call held, where the score projection missed, and which matches deserve tuning attention.

Before kickoffCheck the provider row, kickoff time, and replay lookup hint.
After final whistleReview the completed-match card for actual-result probability and score miss.
Model tuningUse upset checks and large score misses to decide what to recalibrate next.
